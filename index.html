Privacy Statement

Your child's name, email, group, and workout activity data are stored securely using Supabase (cloud-hosted database). This data is used solely for tracking off-season basketball participation within the Fairview Apaches program.

Who can see this data: Coaching staff (full access) and teammates (leaderboard name & points only). Your child's email address is never shown to other players.

Data sharing: Data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or distributed outside the Fairview basketball program.

Data retention: Activity data is retained for the current off-season only. To opt out or request data deletion at any time, contact the head coach.

Contact: If you have questions about data practices, reach out to the Fairview Apaches coaching staff directly.
🏆 Award Bonus Points
Manually award bonus points to a player — streak rewards, standout effort, etc. These are logged as a separate entry and do not count against the daily cap.
